Retrogaming
===========

This week in retrogaming, we see exciting news about classic games and innovations that bring nostalgia to the forefront. From announcements of new toys and gadgets to considerable developments in gaming rights and the celebration of iconic characters, the retrogaming community is buzzing with activity.

News and Innovations
--------------------

Recent advances in gaming nostalgia are highlighted by innovative product releases and surprising comebacks.

- LEGO is set to release a working arcade pinball machine as part of its latest lineup `LEGO PS1 Set Is Dropping This Year <https://retrododo.com/lego-ps1-set-is-dropping-this-year/>`_.
- The Nintendo GameCube can now run Windows NT after a new fan-made project emerged `The Nintendo GameCube Can Now Run Windows NT <https://retrododo.com/the-nintendo-gamecube-can-now-run-windows-nt/>`_.
- In a peculiar twist, Commodore is returning with an unexpected retro flip phone, even stirring controversy with Nintendo hackers demanding $2 million `Nintendo Hackers Demand $2 Million?! + More Retro News! <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IkAYPhAfyZ0>`_.

Character Celebrations
----------------------

This week celebrates the legacy of Sonic the Hedgehog, as the iconic character reaches a notable anniversary.

- Richard Garriott is reclaiming the rights to the celebrated Ultima series, which has been influential since its inception in 1981 `"Finally, The Time Has Come" - Richard Garriott Is Reclaiming The Rights To Ultima <https://www.timeextension.com/news/2026/06/finally-the-time-has-come-richard-garriott-is-reclaiming-the-rights-to-ultima>`_.
- A feature recounts the enduring impact of Sonic the Hedgehog, focusing on favorite levels, music, and personal memories as the character turns 35 `Feature: In The Zone - Our Favourite Sonic The Hedgehog Levels, Music & Memories <https://www.nintendolife.com/features/in-the-zone-our-favourite-sonic-the-hedgehog-levels-music-and-memories>`_.
- The best Sonic games are ranked, as enthusiasts reflect on the legacy of the blue blur `Guide: Best Sonic Games Of All Time <https://www.nintendolife.com/guides/best-sonic-games-of-all-time>`_.
