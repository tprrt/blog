Retrogaming News Digest
========================

This week in retrogaming, we see a blend of new titles, re-releases, and nostalgic remembrances as developers look to rekindle the magic of classic gaming. Here's a roundup of notable articles categorized by theme.

New Releases and Announcements
-------------------------------

Several developers have announced exciting new games that pay homage to classic titles while introducing innovative gameplay elements.

- **Escape 2049** - French developer OrionSoft unveils a cinematic platformer for the Sega Genesis and Game Boy Advance, currently funding via [RetroShell - Independent Retro Gaming News](https://www.retroshell.com).
- **Kidbash: Super Legend** - Acclaim announces a nostalgic roguelike action-platformer, drawing inspiration from Mega Man and Kirby 64, set for a 2027 release ([Nintendo Life | Latest Updates](https://www.nintendolife.com/news/2026/05/acclaim-brings-nostalgic-roguelike-action-platformer-kidbash-to-consoles-in-2027)).
- **Miniaturised Classics** - Homebrew dev Richter Wilker skillfully demakes Castlevania: SoTN and Animal Crossing for Game Boy Color, showcasing incredible fan creativity ([Time Extension | Latest Updates](https://www.timeextension.com/news/2026/05/castlevania-sotn-and-animal-crossing-get-miniaturised-for-game-boy-color)).

-------------------------

The nostalgia wave continues as older titles see physical re-releases and updates, ensuring their legacy remains vibrant.

- **Zool Re-Release** - A physical re-release of Zool is on the cards for the Sega Mega Drive, promising authentic presentation and materials ([Time Extension | Retro Gaming News 24/7](https://www.timeextension.com)).
- **Retro Re-release Roundup** - This week's roundup features a summary of various re-release announcements that focus on classic titles returning to modern platforms ([Retronauts](https://www.retronauts.com/article/2511/retro-re-release-roundup-week-of-may-21-2026)).
- **Hardware Refresh** - New trademark applications from Atari suggest plans for a hardware refresh of their cherished home computers, keeping the vintage spirit alive ([Time Extension | Latest Updates](https://www.timeextension.com/news/2026/05/new-atari-trademark-application-hints-at-hardware-refresh-for-mr-ts-favourite-home-computer)).
