Linux Gaming
============

The Linux gaming scene continues to evolve, with a host of new developments enhancing the overall experience for gamers on the platform. Notable progress has been made in game availability, performance optimizations, and support from major companies like Valve.

Native Game Releases
--------------------

One of the most exciting trends is the increase in native Linux games, alongside ongoing improvements in compatibility with Steam and Proton. This uptick is likely to attract more users to the Linux gaming community.

- `New Steam Games with Native Linux Builds, including Sodaman - 2026-06-03 Edition <https://boilingsteam.com/new-steam-games-with-native-linux-builds-2026-06-03-edition/index.html>`_: 90 new Steam games with native Linux builds released recently.
- `New Steam Games Playable on the Steam Deck, with XF Extreme Formula and Mina the Hollower - 2026-05-30 Edition <https://boilingsteam.com/new-steam-games-playable-on-the-steam-deck-2026-05-30-edition/index.html>`_: 14 newly released games rated as Verified or Playable on the Steam Deck.

Valve’s Initiatives
-------------------

Valve is making strides to enhance the gaming experience on Linux through various platforms and technologies, including their Steam line-up and support frameworks.

- `Valve continue working towards the Steam Frame with a new SteamVR Beta <https://www.gamingonlinux.com/2026/06/valve-continue-working-towards-the-steam-frame-with-a-new-steamvr-beta/>`_: Updates and improvements to SteamVR for Linux users.
- `Steam Machine and Steam Frame shipping "this summer", Valve now rolling out verification systems <https://www.gamingonlinux.com/2026/06/steam-machine-and-steam-frame-shipping-this-summer-valve-now-rolling-out-verification-systems/>`_: Valve announces the shipping of Steam Machines and Frame systems soon.

Performance and Hardware Support
--------------------------------

Improving hardware support and performance optimizations are crucial for enhancing gaming on Linux. Recent benchmarks and updates show positive progress in these areas.

- `Linux Performance, Benchmarks & Open-Source News - Phoronix <https://www.phoronix.com/linux/Linux+Gaming>`_: SDL's upgrades aim to enhance gaming performance on Linux.
- `Linux 7.2 Continues Improving AMDGPU Support On POWER, ARM <https://www.phoronix.com/news/Linux-7.2-AMDGPU-Non-4K>`_: The introduction of HDMI 2.1 FRL support boosts AMDGPU performance.
- `Benchmarking The BORE Scheduler Performance With CachyOS Linux <https://www.phoronix.com/review/cachyos-bore>`_: Performance assessments of different Linux kernel flavors reveal significant insights.

Community and Resources
-----------------------

Engaging with the Linux gaming community and accessing resources can greatly enhance a user's experience. Various platforms are contributing to the growing ecosystem.

- `Linux Game Consortium - News, Reviews, and Proton Gaming <https://linuxgameconsortium.com>`_: A comprehensive source for Linux gaming news, reviews, and accessories.
- `Why everyone suddenly cares about Linux gaming—and it's not just the Steam Deck <https://www.howtogeek.com/why-everyone-suddenly-cares-about-linux-gamingand-its-not-just-the-steam-deck>`_: An insightful article exploring the surge in interest around Linux gaming. 

Desktop Environment Updates
---------------------------

Improving the desktop environments is pivotal for an optimal gaming experience. Recent updates in popular Linux environments are also focused on enhancing user experience.

- `KDE Plasma 6.8 Will Make Sure You Don't Miss Your Low Battery Notifications While Gaming <https://www.phoronix.com/news/Plasma-6.8-Low-Battery-Full-S>`_: Upcoming features aim to refine user notifications during gaming sessions.
- `The new Steam Store home page is here with improved gamepad navigation <https://www.gamingonlinux.com/2026/06/the-new-steam-store-home-page-is-here-with-improved-gamepad-navigation/>`_: Enhancements for better usability on Steam Deck and gamepad navigation.
